History & Mission
Dr.Chef provides nutritious balanced vegetarian lunches to school children aged between 5 and 17 years. Our Mission is provide Indain food which is prepared from local vegetables and fruits. Our meals are packed with the nutrients that are required for a growing child’s needs. We take care of the whole process - cooking, packing and delivering the kids meals straight to the children’s classrooms.
